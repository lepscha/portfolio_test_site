    Get to know me

    After years spent building a successful career in finance, Jacob Hensley still felt that something was missing. Looking towards a career change, his thoughts were constantly drawn to his lifelong passion for and wealth of experience with animals. His time in the demanding world of finance gave Jacob a deep understanding of how important it is for busy owners to know their pets are getting the best possible care, attention, and exercise. Drawing on his experience walking, breeding, and training dogs, Jacob set out to create a pet care company that would blend reliable and professional service with a personal touch. He founded District Dogs in 2014.
